About

Welcome to my personal webpage for ICS2O-A, it is designed as an experiment for me to apply what I have learned throughout the course. The course has introduced me to computer programming and its concepts, by learning how to create clear maintainable code and documenting. I also learned how to manage a computer by studying hardware configurations, software selection, operating systems, networking, and performing safe computing practices. There was also an investigation of the social impacts that computers make, as well as the development and understanding of enviornmental and ethical issues related to the use of computers.

That being said, the course has inspired me to explore more about computers and I learned that the domain is quite broad, after further investigation, I was fascinated with the overlap of math and computer science, such as algorithms and data structures. I had no experience with computers and programming prior to the course, so at first I was a bit overwhelmed with the syntax of Ruby's language and setting up the enviornment. However, after a long period of trial and error, programming started to become more fun and I started to spend more time with programming and I learned more from other programmers on online communities.
